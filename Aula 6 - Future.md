# Future (Futuro)

## 1. O que é o Future?

O **Future** é usado para falar sobre:

* Ações que vão acontecer
* Planos
* Decisões
* Previsões

### Exemplos:

* I will study tomorrow.
* She is going to work tonight.
* We will deploy the system.

---

## 2. Regra principal (forma do verbo)

No futuro, o **verbo principal permanece na forma base (sem alteração)**.

### Forma base:

* work
* study
* fix
* deploy

### Exemplos:

* I will work
* She is going to study

---

## 3. Future com "Will"

### Uso:

* Decisões no momento
* Promessas
* Previsões

---

### Estrutura afirmativa

**Sujeito + will + verbo base**

* I will work
* She will study
* We will deploy
* He will fix the bug

---

### Forma do verbo:

* "will" indica o futuro
* O verbo principal fica na **forma base**

---

### Contração:

* will → 'll

Exemplos:

* I'll work
* She'll study

---

### Negativa:

**Sujeito + will not (won’t) + verbo base**

* I will not work
* She won’t study

---

### Pergunta:

**Will + sujeito + verbo base?**

* Will you work?
* Will she study?

---

### Respostas:

* Yes, I will.
* No, I won’t.

---

## 4. Future com "Going To"

### Uso:

* Planos já definidos
* Intenção
* Decisões anteriores

---

### Estrutura afirmativa

**Sujeito + to be (am/is/are) + going to + verbo base**

* I am going to work
* She is going to study
* They are going to deploy

---

### Forma do verbo:

* O tempo está no **verbo to be (am / is / are)**
* "going to" indica futuro
* O verbo principal permanece na **forma base**

---

### Contrações:

* I am → I'm
* You are → You're
* He is → He's

---

### Negativa:

**Sujeito + to be + not + going to + verbo base**

* I am not going to work
* She isn’t going to study

---

### Pergunta:

**To be + sujeito + going to + verbo base?**

* Are you going to work?
* Is she going to study?

---

### Respostas:

* Yes, I am.
* No, I’m not.

---

## 5. Diferença entre "Will" e "Going To"

| Uso                | Will       | Going to     |
| ------------------ | ---------- | ------------ |
| Decisão            | Na hora    | Já planejada |
| Tipo de ação       | Espontânea | Intencional  |
| Estrutura do verbo | Base       | Base         |

### Exemplos:

* I will help you (decisão no momento)
* I am going to help you (já planejado)

---

## 6. Palavras comuns

* Tomorrow
* Next week
* Later
* Soon

### Exemplos:

* I will study tomorrow
* We are going to deploy next week

---

## 7. Exemplos do dia a dia (TI)

* I will fix the bug
* We will deploy the system
* I am going to study React
* They are going to build an application
* We will update the database

---

## 8. Erros comuns

❌ I will worked → ✔ I will work
❌ I will working → ✔ I will work
❌ I am going work → ✔ I am going to work
❌ She is going to studying → ✔ She is going to study

---

## 10. Dica importante

Se houver "will" ou "going to":

* O verbo principal permanece na forma base

### Exemplos:

* I will work
* I am going to study

---

## 11. Resumo final

* O futuro é indicado por "will" ou "going to"
* O verbo principal não muda
* O tempo não está no verbo principal
* O tempo está na estrutura (will / to be + going to)
