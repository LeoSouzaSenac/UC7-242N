# Simple Past (Passado Simples)

## 1. O que é o Simple Past?

O **Simple Past** é usado para falar sobre:

* Ações que **já aconteceram**
* Situações finalizadas no passado
* Eventos com tempo definido

### Exemplos:

* I worked yesterday.
* She studied last night.
* They deployed the system.

---

## 2. Estrutura básica

### Frase afirmativa

**Sujeito + verbo no passado**

| Sujeito | Verbo   | Exemplo               |
| ------- | ------- | --------------------- |
| I       | worked  | I worked yesterday    |
| You     | studied | You studied a lot     |
| He      | fixed   | He fixed the bug      |
| She     | watched | She watched the video |
| They    | played  | They played soccer    |

---

## 3. Verbos regulares (ED)

### Regra geral:

Adicionar **-ed**

* work → worked
* play → played

---

### Verbos terminados em "e"

Adicionar apenas **-d**

* like → liked
* use → used

---

### Verbos terminados em consoante + y

Troca o **y** por **ied**

* study → studied
* try → tried

---

### Verbos curtos (CVC)

Dobra a última letra + **ed**

* stop → stopped
* plan → planned

---

## 4. Verbos irregulares

Não seguem padrão — precisam ser memorizados.

| Presente | Passado |
| -------- | ------- |
| go       | went    |
| make     | made    |
| have     | had     |
| see      | saw     |
| come     | came    |
| take     | took    |

### Exemplos:

* I went to work
* She made a project
* They had a meeting

---

## 5. Frases negativas

### Estrutura:

**Sujeito + did not (didn't) + verbo base**

| Sujeito | Forma negativa | Exemplo               |
| ------- | -------------- | --------------------- |
| I       | did not        | I did not work        |
| You     | didn't         | You didn't study      |
| He      | didn't         | He didn't fix the bug |

---

## 6. Frases interrogativas

### Estrutura:

**Did + sujeito + verbo base?**

### Exemplos:

* Did you work?
* Did she study?
* Did they play?

---

## 7. Perguntas com WH-

### Estrutura:

**WH + did + sujeito + verbo base?**

### Exemplos:

* What did you do?
* Where did she go?
* Why did they stop?

---

## 8. Respostas

### Curtas:

* Yes, I did.
* No, I didn't.

### Longas:

* Yes, I worked.
* No, I didn't work.

---

## 9. Palavras comuns (tempo)

* Yesterday (ontem)
* Last night (noite passada)
* Last week (semana passada)
* Ago (atrás)

### Exemplos:

* I studied yesterday
* She worked last night
* They deployed the system last week

---

## 10. Uso do verbo "to be" no passado

| Sujeito           | Forma |
| ----------------- | ----- |
| I / He / She / It | was   |
| You / We / They   | were  |

### Exemplos:

* I was tired
* She was at work
* They were happy

---

### Negativa:

* I was not (wasn't)
* They were not (weren't)

---

### Pergunta:

* Was she at work?
* Were they ready?

---

## 11. Diferença: verbo normal vs "to be"

* I worked yesterday (ação)
* I was tired yesterday (estado)

---

## 12. Exemplos do dia a dia (TI)

* I fixed a bug
* We deployed the system
* They tested the application
* She updated the database
* The server crashed

---

## 13. Erros comuns

❌ I did worked → ✔ I did work
❌ Did you worked? → ✔ Did you work?
❌ She didn't studied → ✔ She didn't study
