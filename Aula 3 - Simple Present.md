# Simple Present (Presente Simples) - Guia Completo

## 1. O que é o Simple Present?

O **Simple Present** é usado para falar sobre:

* Hábitos
* Rotinas
* Verdades universais
* Fatos

### Exemplos:

* I work every day.
* She studies at night.
* The sun rises in the east.

---

## 2. Estrutura básica

### Frase afirmativa

**Sujeito + verbo (forma base)**

| Sujeito | Verbo | Exemplo          |
| ------- | ----- | ---------------- |
| I       | work  | I work every day |
| You     | study | You study a lot  |
| We      | play  | We play soccer   |
| They    | eat   | They eat pizza   |

---

## 3. Regra do "S" (he, she, it)

Quando usamos:

* He
* She
* It

O verbo ganha **S**

### Exemplos:

* He works
* She studies
* It runs

---

### Regras especiais:

#### Verbos terminados em:

* s
* sh
* ch
* x
* o

adiciona **ES**

Exemplos:

* He watches
* She goes
* It fixes

#### Verbos terminados em consoante + y

troca o **y** por **ies**

Exemplos:

* study → studies
* try → tries

---

## 4. Frases negativas

### Estrutura:

**Sujeito + do/does + not + verbo base**

| Sujeito       | Auxiliar           | Exemplo           |
| ------------- | ------------------ | ----------------- |
| I/You/We/They | do not (don't)     | I don't work      |
| He/She/It     | does not (doesn't) | She doesn't study |

---

## 5. Frases interrogativas (perguntas)

### Estrutura:

**Do/Does + sujeito + verbo base?**

Exemplos:

* Do you work?
* Does she study?
* Do they play?

---

## 6. Respostas curtas

### Positivas:

* Yes, I do.
* Yes, she does.

### Negativas:

* No, I don't.
* No, she doesn't.

---

## 7. Uso com frequência (advérbios)

Palavras comuns:

* Always (sempre)
* Usually (geralmente)
* Often (frequentemente)
* Sometimes (às vezes)
* Never (nunca)

### Exemplos:

* I always study.
* She usually works.
* They never eat vegetables.

---

## 8. Exemplos do dia a dia

* I work with technology.
* I teach students.
* I drink coffee.
* She watches TV.
* They play games.

---

## 9. Erros comuns

❌ She work → ✔ She works
❌ He don't study → ✔ He doesn't study
❌ Does she studies? → ✔ Does she study?

---
