# Present Continuous (Presente Contínuo)

## 1. O que é o Present Continuous?

O **Present Continuous** é usado para falar sobre:

* Ações acontecendo **agora**
* Situações **temporárias**
* Ações em progresso
* Planos futuros já definidos

### Exemplos:

* I am studying now.
* She is working at the moment.
* They are building a system.
* We are meeting the client tomorrow.

---

## 2. Estrutura básica

### Frase afirmativa

**Sujeito + verbo to be + verbo com ING**

| Sujeito | To be | Verbo + ing | Exemplo                    |
| ------- | ----- | ----------- | -------------------------- |
| I       | am    | working     | I am working now           |
| You     | are   | studying    | You are studying English   |
| He      | is    | coding      | He is coding               |
| She     | is    | reading     | She is reading             |
| It      | is    | running     | It is running              |
| We      | are   | developing  | We are developing a system |
| They    | are   | playing     | They are playing           |

---

## 3. Contrações (forma natural)

| Forma completa | Contração |
| -------------- | --------- |
| I am           | I'm       |
| You are        | You're    |
| He is          | He's      |
| She is         | She's     |
| It is          | It's      |
| We are         | We're     |
| They are       | They're   |

### Exemplos:

* I'm working now
* She's studying
* We're developing a system

---

## 4. Regras do ING

### Regra geral:

Adicionar **-ing**

* work → working
* play → playing

---

### Verbos terminados em "e"

Remove o **e** e adiciona **-ing**

* make → making
* write → writing

---

### Verbos curtos (CVC)

Consoante + vogal + consoante → dobra a última letra

* run → running
* sit → sitting

---

## 5. Frases negativas

### Estrutura:

**Sujeito + to be + not + verbo com ING**

| Sujeito | Forma negativa   | Exemplo             |
| ------- | ---------------- | ------------------- |
| I       | am not           | I am not working    |
| You     | are not (aren’t) | You aren't studying |
| He      | is not (isn't)   | He isn't coding     |

---

## 6. Frases interrogativas

### Estrutura:

**To be + sujeito + verbo com ING?**

### Exemplos:

* Are you working?
* Is she studying?
* Are they playing?

---

## 7. Perguntas com WH-

### Estrutura:

**WH + to be + sujeito + verbo com ING?**

### Exemplos:

* What are you doing?
* Where are they working?
* Why is she studying?

---

## 8. Respostas

### Curtas:

* Yes, I am.
* No, I am not.

### Longas:

* Yes, I am working.
* No, I am not working.

---

## 9. Palavras comuns (tempo)

* Now (agora)
* At the moment (no momento)
* Right now (agora mesmo)

### Exemplos:

* I am studying now.
* She is working at the moment.

---

## 10. Uso para futuro (planejamento)

O Present Continuous pode indicar **futuro já combinado**:

* I am meeting the client tomorrow
* We are deploying tonight

---

## 11. Verbos que NÃO usam ING (Stative Verbs)

Esses verbos não são usados no Present Continuous:

* know (saber)
* want (querer)
* like (gostar)
* love (amar)
* hate (odiar)
* need (precisar)
* believe (acreditar)
* understand (entender)

### Exemplos:

❌ I am knowing the answer
✔ I know the answer

❌ She is liking pizza
✔ She likes pizza

---

## 12. Diferença: estado vs ação

Alguns verbos mudam de sentido:

* I think (opinião)
* I am thinking (pensando agora)

👉 Isso é importante pra evitar confusão.

---

## 13. Diferença: Simple Present vs Present Continuous

* I work every day. (rotina)
* I am working now. (agora)

---

## 14. Exemplos do dia a dia (TI)

* I am coding a new feature
* We are fixing a bug
* They are testing the system
* She is updating the database
* We are deploying the application

---

## 15. Erros comuns

❌ I working → ✔ I am working
❌ She is work → ✔ She is working
❌ Are you work? → ✔ Are you working?
❌ I am knowing → ✔ I know
